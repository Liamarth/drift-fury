Informations

Description:

Drift Fury is a small, endless car-drifitng game. You can buy uprgades, buy cars,
create custom ones and compete with all players.

Controls:

Use left arrow key or A to turn left, and right arrow key or D to turn left.

Assets:

All Assets, as the pixel art, the music or the font were made by my own.

Tips & Tricks:

Buy your favourite car and then invest the money into upgrades, so you get a higher highscore,
earn more money and become the best in the leaderboard.
